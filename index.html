A LITTLE BIT ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Strahinja and I'm a web developer with passion for front end web development and design. I graduated from Cleveland State University with a degree in Computer Engineering. I have always enjoyed programming because it stimulated the more technical side of my brain but it left the creative side lacking a bit. That changed when I started dabbling with web design. I have always appreciated art in its many different forms and a beautifully designed website is art in some form. I thrive on the intersection of technology and aesthetics, and I find immense satisfaction in crafting visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces.

When I'm not sitting on my computer, I enjoy riding my motorcycles, reading, and playing video games.
